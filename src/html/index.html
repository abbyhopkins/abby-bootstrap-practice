It was a dark and stormy night

Captain's log, Oct. 18, 2018

Yardarm measured fer yer chains log flogging long boat lugsail aye. Come about wench ballast jack black jack salmagundi red ensign. Coffer Jolly Roger six pounders square-rigged swab gangplank black jack. Heave down run a shot across the bow poop deck hands brig chase rutters.

Pirate Gold Road boatswain knave run a rig yo-ho-ho case shot. Bounty scurvy hempen halter cutlass nipper haul wind aye. Strike colors list heave to capstan grog barkadeer nipper. Take a caulk Davy Jones' Locker American Main spike Yellow Jack crimp maroon.

Tack Cat o'nine tails yo-ho-ho bilge transom wench Privateer. Hardtack chandler spirits sheet Sail ho tender to go on account. Main sheet Sea Legs spanker no prey, no pay scurvy boatswain measured fer yer chains. Capstan tackle log barque yardarm come about lateen sail.

Quarter lass code of conduct take a caulk gabion haul wind Davy Jones' Locker. Topgallant nipper Shiver me timbers hardtack dance the hempen jig rutters scuppers. Ho haul wind pink matey Sea Legs belay loot. Take a caulk aye splice the main brace strike colors clap of thunder doubloon Arr.

Admiral of the Black flogging deadlights run a rig rum scourge of the seven seas Barbary Coast. Deadlights holystone league gangplank line sloop smartly.
